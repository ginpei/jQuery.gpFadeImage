jQuery.gpFadeImage

The plugin that change images with fade effect.

for: IE 6+, Firefox, Chrome, Safari, Opera

$(elem).gpFadeImage([
  'image02.jpg',
  'image03.jpg',
  'image04.jpg'
]);

See sample codes in HTML source,
and more informations in: Ginpen.com.
